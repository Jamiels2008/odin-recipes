Baked Chicken

Description

This recipe combines exquisite taste with simplicity. It is an easy-to-make, yet delicious meal full of protein and carbs to fuel your day.

Ingredients:

  • chicken breasts
  • white rice
  • garlic salt
  • fajita chicken rub
  • grape seed oil spray

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Prepare chicken
    1. cut fat off breasts
    2. place them on baking tray lined with foil
    3. spray with grape seed oil
    4. lightly cover with garlic salt
    5. generously cover with fajita chicken rub
    6. place in oven for 40 mins
  3. Prepare white rice
    1. wash 3 cups of white rice, then add to pot
    2. add 4 cups of cold water
    3. turn burner on High until boiling, then place lid on and turn to low for 11mins
  4. allow chicken to sit for 5mins after pulling from oven to continue cooking
  5. serve 225g of white rice and 6oz of chicken and enjoy!
